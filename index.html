Our Mission

Closing the language gap in AI


Africa is home to over 2,000 languages — yet the vast majority remain underrepresented in modern AI systems. AfriAnnotate exists to change that.

We combine native-speaker annotators with AI-assisted workflows to deliver enterprise-grade labeled datasets for NLP, speech, and multimodal AI applications.

Named Entity Recognition Sentiment Analysis Translation Pairs Speech Transcription Intent Classification Machine Reading

"Building the data infrastructure that gives African languages a voice in the age of AI."
