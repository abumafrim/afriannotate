Our Mission

Africa is home to over 2,000 languages — yet the vast majority remain invisible to modern AI systems. AfriAnnotate exists to close that gap. We combine native-speaker annotators with AI-assisted workflows to deliver enterprise-grade labeled data at scale.

Founded in 2024, our team spans linguists, engineers, and NLP researchers working together to make African languages first-class citizens of the AI world.
